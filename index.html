Daan Smit
I have a smallish room for multiple activities - home office; sewing; crafts; and sometimes it also needs to be a bedroom for visitors. I bought this table for crafting projects. I like it because:

1. The size (4 feet) doesn’t take up too much space, but it’s big enough to be usable.
2. It’s reasonably stable - yes, there’s a slight wobble if you try to make it wobble, but I won’t be doing anything that requires a rock-solid surface.

3. It can easily be folded up and stored when I want to change the room into a bedroom.
4. Best of all, the leg braces are designed in such a way that I can put plastic drawer units under it. I have two units from the craft store under it. One is 15”w x 15”d x 23.5” high. The other is 12”w x 15”d x 24” high. No drawers are blocked by the braces. There is enough space between the two units that I can roll my desk chair up to it and work in perfect comfort. (See my photos.)
5. I was very happy to see that the legs are gray, not chrome, since my drawer units are gray.
SUPER DUPER TABLE - my collegas envy me every day
